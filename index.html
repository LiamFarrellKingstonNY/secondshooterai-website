Features

Everything you need on set.

Built from the ground up for working wedding photographers who need to be present, confident, and creative on every shoot.

Live AI Direction

Real-time AI-generated posing directions tailored to your scene — golden hour portraits, ceremony, first dance, and more. Suggestions sound natural, not scripted.

Hands-Free Voice Cues

Keep your camera to your eye. Second Shooter speaks directions aloud through your earpiece so you never have to look down at your phone mid-shoot.

Custom Pose Library

Save the directions that feel authentically yours. Build your personal pose vocabulary and access your entire custom library instantly during any session.

Smart Session Setup

Tap a few chips — session type, mood, duration — and Second Shooter tunes its AI suggestions to your exact scenario before you fire a single frame.

Session History & Replay

Review every direction from every shoot. See exactly which prompts generated your best images and learn from each session to sharpen your directing over time.

PDF Session Summaries

Export a full session summary as a shareable PDF — poses used, timing, and your saved favorites — to review between jobs and refine your workflow.

How It Works

Three steps to better direction.

Second Shooter is built to disappear into your workflow — so all you have to think about is your light.

01
1

Set Up Your Session

Tell Second Shooter what you're shooting — wedding ceremony, engagement portraits, reception. Pick your mood, style, and duration. The AI tunes itself in seconds.

02
2

Start Shooting

As you photograph, the app surfaces natural, human-sounding directions you can speak directly to your couples. Tap for the next one, save the ones that land.

03
3

Review & Save

After the shoot, see exactly what you directed and when. Save your favorite prompts to build a growing personal library that gets better with every wedding.
